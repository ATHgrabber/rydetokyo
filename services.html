We recommend booking at least 24 hours in advance. For peak season (March–May, October–November) and weekends, 2–3 days ahead is ideal. Same-day requests are possible but subject to availability — just message us early.
Cancel free of charge up to 24 hours before your scheduled delivery. Cancellations within 24 hours are charged 50% of the rental fee. No-shows are charged the full rate.
No deposit is required. Payment is collected on the day of delivery (cash or card). We ask for your hotel name and room number as confirmation.
Normal wear is covered. In the event of an accident or significant damage, costs will be assessed based on the repair needed. We strongly recommend taking our optional damage coverage — see the Insurance page for details.
