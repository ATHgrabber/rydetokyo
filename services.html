Good to know

We recommend booking at least 24 hours in advance. For peak season (March–May, October–November) and weekends, 2–3 days ahead is ideal. Same-day requests are possible but subject to availability — just message us early.

No deposit is required. Payment is collected on the day of delivery (cash or card). We ask for your hotel name and room number as confirmation.
