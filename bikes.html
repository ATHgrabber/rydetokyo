Why we chose this bike

The Escape R E+ is the only premium city e-bike currently produced in Japan. The SyncDrive Core motor (co-developed with Yamaha) is quiet, responsive, and perfectly tuned for flat urban riding. The 200km ECO range means you'll never run out on a day trip. We've fitted every bike with puncture-resistant Schwalbe Marathon tyres — so you can ride without worrying.
